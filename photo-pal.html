Photo-Pal

Photo-Pal is a location-based image sharing app that enables users to discover locations in their area or when they are planning trips for photography

Project Background

As part of the CareerFoundry UI Design course I put together the following Case Study.

The casestudy was put together over a period of three months using tools like Figma, Usability Hub, as well as good old fashioned pen and papper.

The 5 W's

Who?

The app is for people who want to discover new locations to explore, view/upload/share photos, as well as engage with other users.

What?

Users will be able to discover new picturesque locations, see photos other users have uploaded for inspiration, and engage with other like-minded users.

When?

Users will engage with the app when they are planning trips, seeking inspiration, looking for events/communities, or when they want to upload and share photos with other users.

Where?

The app will be used at home while planning a trip, seeking inspiration, or looking for local communities/events. Also while on holidays to find locations of interest, or to upload and share photos.

Why?

Users will choose to use this app because it is a dedicated location-based photo app which will help them find inspiration and locations, as well as upload and share images efficiently. The app will also have community/social functionality to allow users to plan group events or message each other.

Research

A hypothesis was put together the captured what we thought the user wants the app to do, the hypothesis:

'Beginner to experienced photographers want an app to help in the discovery of new locations and planning trips. They also want to socialise by sharing photos with other users, viewing photos taken by other users, and messaging like-minded people in their free time.'
